About Scawwy Howwow

Inspired by the theatre manifesto of Antonin Artaud, Scawwy Howwow Theatre aims to push the limits of theatre, media, and horror. From new formats to new mediums to new voices, we aim to bring a whole new experience to the world. For inquiries, reach us at scawwyhowwowtheatre@gmail.com.
